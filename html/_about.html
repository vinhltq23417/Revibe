Transparency You Can Feel. Stories You Can Trust.

ReVibe was founded on one principle: to build a trusted bridge between you and the real story behind every sustainable product.

Tired of Greenwashing?

The market is full of vague 'eco-friendly' claims. Consumers have lost trust, confused by 'greenwashing' and brands that don't share the full story. We knew there had to be a better way.

At the same time, mass-market e-commerce platforms can feel soulless. We crave a connection to the products we buy, but we're left with no story, no soul, and no proof of sustainable practice.

The Rebirth Journey

We show you the full story. From the raw, recycled materials to the artisan's hands, you see the entire documented journey of your product's creation.

The Sustainability Score

No more vague claims. We provide a clear, data-driven score for every item based on verified criteria, so you can shop with true confidence.

Our Partners in Sustainability

We don't just sell products, we partner with local creators, makers, and artisans who share our values. They are the soul of ReVibe.

The Saigon Upcyclers

Location: Ho Chi Minh City

Materials: Recycled Banners, PVC

Led by Kien, they see the city's discarded advertising banners as a canvas of history, crafting them into one-of-a-kind bags.

Vung Tau Reclaimed

Location: Vung Tau

Materials: Reclaimed Boat Wood

Founded by Mr. Ba, this workshop gives old fishing boats a new life, preserving the wood's original paint and patina.

Hanoi Circular Textiles

Location: Hanoi

Materials: Recycled Denim, PET Bottles

Linh Bui's workshop tackles fashion's waste by re-spinning post-consumer jeans into verifiably sustainable new apparel.

Tire Vibe

Location: Da Nang

Materials: Recycled Tires

Where most see waste, Khanh sees potential. His team transforms rugged, non-biodegradable rubber into sleek wallets and belts.

Our Commitment Goes Further

Sustainability isn't just a label on a product. It's a promise that runs through every part of our operation.

Carbon-Neutral Shipping
